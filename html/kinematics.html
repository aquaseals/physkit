When typing in the direction of vectors follow this format to get the right answers! (otherwise answer is always 0 lol)

Note: letters can be typed in upper or lower case!

  1. Type in Magnitude

  2. Vector at an Angle?

    1. Type in starting direction, N/E/S/W, then space
    2. Type in the angle of the vector, space
    3. Type in the direction the angle is going towards form the starting point N/E/S/W

    Note: Make sure your directions are perpendicular to each other (e.x. N 30 S is not an eligible angle)

  3. Direction just N/E/S/W?

    Type in direction, N/E/S/W

Wrong: S30W or 90 SW or E 90 W

Correct: e 20 N or S 63.3 E or w 42 n
